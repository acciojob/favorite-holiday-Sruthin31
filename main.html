Paris


Paris, often called The City of Light, is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. It is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, world-class art museums, and charming street cafés. Whether it’s strolling along the Seine River or admiring the Eiffel Tower at night, Paris offers a magical experience to every visitor.
